Mahindra makes authentic SUVs that help you explore the impossible. Mahindra's SUVs offer comfort, luxury, the latest in tech with the freedom to explore. With its SUV DNA, Mahindra’s lineup blends capability with modern sophistication. From refined on-road performance to legendary off-road prowess - Mahindra SUVs are designed to offer cutting-edge technology, premium features, and a connected driving experience. Built to handle India’s diverse terrains, these SUVs resonate with today’s discerning drivers who value both style and substance.

Mahindra offers a vibrant and evolving color palette that complements the design of each SUV. Popular shades include Pearl White, Red Rage, Midnight Black, Everest White, Galaxy Grey, Nebula Blue, Dazzling Silver, Forest Green, and more. New and special-edition colors are regularly introduced for select models — explore all the available colors to find your perfect match.

Mahindra SUVs such as the XUV700, Scorpio-N, Bolero, Bolero Neo, and the Thar Roxx are designed with spacious interiors, ample legroom, foldable seats, and sufficient cargo space. Some models offer both 6 and 7-seater configurations. Compare seating layouts and variants to find what suits your lifestyle.

Whether you're heading out for a weekend getaway or a long road trip, Mahindra SUVs like the XUV700, Scorpio-N, XUV 3XO, Bolero, and Bolero Neo deliver the ideal combination of comfort, performance, and tech. With plush interiors, multiple seating options, connected features, and top-tier safety, these SUVs redefine travel.

Mahindra SUVs are engineered for confidence, with several models earning 5-star safety ratings from Global NCAP and Bharat NCAP. Other Safety Features include multiple airbags, Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS), Electronic Stability Program (ESP), Anti-lock Braking System (ABS), rear and 360-degree cameras, and other intelligent driver aids. Safety is a priority across Mahindra's entire SUV lineup, helping you drive smarter and safer.

For true off-road performance, look no further than the Thar and Scorpio-N. These models come equipped with advanced 4x4 systems, Mechanical Locking Differential (MLD), Brake Locking Differential (BLD), high ground clearance, and torque-rich powertrains. Whether you're tackling mountain trails or muddy backroads, these SUVs are engineered to conquer.

Mahindra’s diverse SUV portfolio includes popular petrol and diesel-powered SUVs like the Thar ROXX, XUV700, Scorpio-N, Thar, XUV 3XO, Bolero, Bolero Neo, Scorpio Classic and more. On the electric front, Mahindra offers the XUV400. With the recently launched Electric Origin SUVs based on the INGLO platform, Mahindra is leading the transition to sustainable mobility. The current eSUV lineup includes the BE 6 and XEV 9e.
